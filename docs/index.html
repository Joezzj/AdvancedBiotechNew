Product Information

Advanced Biotech’s polymer technology enables antibacterial, antifungal, and—depending on formulation—antiviral and anti-mite performance, while being engineered for human and mammalian safety. We can propose an implementation plan and tailor the formulation to your production needs.

• Electrostatic targeting of microbes. Many bacteria and fungi carry a negatively charged cell surface. Our positively charged antibacterial polymers are attracted to these surfaces, then disrupt membrane integrity—causing leakage of cellular contents and loss of viability.

• Selectivity for mammalian cells. Mammalian cell membranes are near-neutral overall, which reduces non-specific binding of cationic polymers under normal use conditions. This difference in surface charge underpins the technology’s selectivity.

• Process-friendly. The polymers exhibit high thermal stability and elevated decomposition temperatures, making them compatible with common molding and compounding processes.

Antibacterial Masterbatch

Crafted using molecular-level engineering, this product integrates a polymeric organic antibacterial agent to deliver excellent performance against bacteria, viruses, molds, and mites. It contains no silver, eliminating concerns about discoloration or metal corrosion, making it safe to embed into diverse materials. Widely applied across food packaging, textiles, livestock, and water purification, it sustains stable efficacy over extended periods. Customization is available to tailor it to your application needs.

Antibacterial Liquid Concentrate

A versatile and high-strength antibacterial concentrate, this product contains polymeric organic actives designed to provide effective protection. In its liquid form, it integrates seamlessly into existing blending and filling lines, allowing manufacturers to easily incorporate it into their production processes. It delivers consistent antibacterial function across a wide range of products, ensuring reliable performance in diverse applications.
