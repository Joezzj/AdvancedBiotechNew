Our Products

High-quality antibacterial functional materials for various industries

Antibacterial Masterbatch

Engineered for powerful antibacterial performance and excellent processability, this technology integrates easily with a wide range of resins and manufacturing methods.

99.9% antibacterial efficacy (ISO-tested) compatible with PP, ABS, Nylon, PE, PET, and other plastics.

Supports injection, extrusion, spinning, and blow molding.

Safe & eco-friendly: non-leaching and harmless to humans, animals, and water systems.

Customizable: base material, active content, color, and additives tailored to your needs.

Antibacterial Liquid Concentrate

Uniformly dispersed for reliable antibacterial performance and easy customization across applications.

High concentration: Effective even when diluted.

Versatile platform: Ideal for sprays, coatings, and cleaning products.

Easy integration: Fits seamlessly into existing production lines.

Safe & eco-friendly: Free of silver and harmful metals.

Antibacterial Liquid / Disinfectant Spray

Formulated with non-silver, low-odor, and non-toxic technology, this antibacterial solution provides fast, broad-spectrum protection against bacteria and viruses.

Ideal for public spaces, schools, offices, and factories, it can be customized for spray or surface cleaning applications to enhance hygiene and improve cleaning efficiency while minimizing environmental impact.

Cleaning Solution for Food Processing Sites

Designed for food processing plants, cafeterias, and kitchens, this solution targets grease, bacteria, and mold in high-hygiene environments.

Our antibacterial functional mother liquid works with cleaning agents to remove grease, disinfect surfaces, and prevent mold growth — all without silver compounds, avoiding corrosion or discoloration.

Daily Cleaning Solution

Maintaining hygiene in schools, offices, and public facilities requires efficient daily cleaning.

Our antibacterial liquid enhances regular cleaning solutions by providing antibacterial and deodorizing effects, while remaining non-silver, low-odor, and non-toxic.

Formulations can be adjusted to suit specific applications, improving both cleaning efficiency and hygiene standards without harming materials or equipment.

Wet Wipes and Sanitary Cloths

Our antibacterial solution provides strong, long-lasting protection for woven and nonwoven fabrics.

It’s water-based, low-irritation, and available in both alcohol and non-alcohol formulas.

Can be combined with moisturizing or fragrance ingredients to enhance comfort and hygiene.

Antibacterial Coating Agent

Our antibacterial coating forms a durable, transparent layer that inhibits bacteria and virus growth on frequently touched surfaces such as doorknobs, handrails, and counters.

It offers excellent water and abrasion resistance, is easy to reapply, and helps maintain long-term cleanliness and hygiene in public and commercial spaces.

Pet Supplies

Our antibacterial masterbatch keeps pet bowls, litter boxes, toys, and brushes clean and odor-free for long periods.

It’s compatible with PE, PP, TPE, and ABS, suitable for injection molding, extrusion, and blow molding.

The formula is non-silver, odorless, and safe for pets.

OEM customization is available for color and concentration adjustments.

Livestock Facilities

Our antibacterial masterbatch helps maintain hygiene in feeders, tanks, flooring, and breeding equipment, reducing bacteria and odor buildup.

It also suppresses ammonia and hydrogen sulfide, improving air quality and the overall environment.

Highly moldable and safe for animals, it can be easily integrated into existing equipment.

OEM customization is available for additional functions and formulations.

Automotive and Home Textiles

Our antibacterial material provides long-lasting protection for textiles and resin products used in items such as car seats, steering wheel covers, sofas, and curtains.

It offers excellent dispersion during processing (extrusion, spinning, or casting), ensuring even antibacterial performance.

The material is durable, resistant to daily wear and washing, and helps enhance the value and longevity of textile products.

Water Purification Equipment

Our antibacterial masterbatch delivers long-lasting protection for water filters, tanks, and distribution systems without affecting water quality.

The non-silver, non-leaching formulation prevents metal corrosion and ensures stable antibacterial performance over time.

It offers high durability and thermal stability, making it ideal for injection molding and blow molding applications.

Food Packaging

Our antibacterial masterbatch combines safety and freshness for food packaging films, trays, and containers.

Formulated with non-silver, inorganic ingredients, it provides strong antibacterial protection while preventing migration into food.

The non-leaching design ensures long-term stability and preserves food quality, supporting both hygiene and extended shelf life.

It’s an ideal solution for maintaining freshness and enhancing the value of food brands.
